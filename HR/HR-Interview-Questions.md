# 📝 Final Tip

For answering behavioral questions, use:

STAR Method:
- S – Situation
- T – Task
- A – Action
- R – Result

Keep answers:
- Honest
- Structured
- Short (45–90 seconds)
- Confident

This taught me the importance of communication and teamwork.

During project submission week, multiple bugs appeared close to deadline.

---

In one project, I noticed performance issues.

Even though it wasn’t assigned to me, I optimized database queries and improved response time significantly.
