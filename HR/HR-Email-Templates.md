# 🧠 Email Writing Rules

✔ Keep emails short and professional  
✔ Use clear subject lines  
✔ Avoid grammar mistakes  
✔ Always attach resume in PDF format  
✔ Avoid emojis in professional emails  
✔ Reply within 24 hours
