# 💬 HR / Recruiter / Employee Messaging Templates  
(For LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, Wellfound, Email, etc.)

---

# 📌 1️⃣ Connecting With HR on LinkedIn (Before Applying)

```
Hello [Name],

I came across your profile while exploring opportunities at [Company Name].

I am a Junior Full Stack Developer skilled in React and Node.js, and I am very interested in potential openings at your organization.

I would appreciate connecting with you.

Thank you.
– Ashish Pawar
```

---

# 📌 2️⃣ After Sending Connection Request (Short Message)

```
Hello [Name],

Thank you for connecting.

I am actively looking for a Junior Full Stack Developer role. If there are any suitable openings, I would be grateful for your guidance.

Thank you for your time.
```

# 📌 3️⃣ Direct Message to HR for Job Inquiry

```
Hello [Name],

I hope you are doing well.

I noticed that your company is hiring for a Junior Developer role. I have experience in React, Node.js, and MongoDB, along with full-stack project development.

I would love the opportunity to discuss how I can contribute.

May I share my resume with you?

Thank you.
```

---

# 📌 4️⃣ Message After Applying for Job

```
Hello [Name],

I recently applied for the Junior Developer position at [Company Name].

I am very interested in this opportunity and would appreciate any updates regarding my application.

Thank you for your time.
```

---

# 📌 5️⃣ Asking Employee for Referral

```
Hello [Name],

I hope you are doing well.

I noticed that you are working at [Company Name]. I recently applied for the Junior Developer role and found it very aligned with my skills.

If possible, I would sincerely appreciate a referral. I can share my resume for your review.

Thank you for your support.
```

---

# 📌 6️⃣ Follow-Up Message (If No Response)

```

I hope you are doing well.

I just wanted to follow up on my previous message regarding the Junior Developer opportunity.

I remain very interested and would appreciate any update when convenient.

Thank you.
```

# 🧠 Messaging Rules (Very Important)

✔ Keep message short (5–8 lines max)  
✔ Be polite  
✔ Never demand referral  
✔ Avoid sending resume without asking  
✔ Personalize message (mention company name)  
✔ Follow up only once (after 4–7 days)  

---

# 🚀 Final Strategy

For job sites like:

- LinkedIn  
- Naukri  
- Indeed  
- Wellfound  
- Internshala  

Always:

1. Apply first  
2. Then message HR  
3. Then try employee referral  
4. Then follow up politely  

Consistency + Professional communication = Higher response rate.
