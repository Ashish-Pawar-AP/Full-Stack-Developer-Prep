# 💰 Salary Negotiation Guide (For Freshers & Junior Developers)

---

# 📌 1️⃣ When Does Salary Discussion Happen?

Salary discussion usually happens:

- After HR round
- During final discussion
- When offer is about to be released
- During recruiter screening call

Always stay calm and professional.

---

# 🎯 2️⃣ Main Rule for Freshers

As a fresher:

✔ Focus on learning  
✔ Focus on growth  
✔ Stay flexible  
✔ Avoid unrealistic expectations  

Do NOT start negotiation aggressively.

---

# 🗣️ 3️⃣ How to Answer: “What Are Your Salary Expectations?”

### Safe Answer (Fresher)

```
As a fresher, my priority is learning and gaining real-world experience. 

I am open to the company’s standard package for this role.
```

---

### If HR Pushes for a Number

```
Based on market research for similar roles, I believe a range of ₹X–₹Y would be fair. 

However, I am open to discussion.
```

Tip: Always give a RANGE, not a fixed number.

---

# 📊 4️⃣ How to Research Salary

Before interview:

- Check Glassdoor
- Check AmbitionBox
- Ask seniors
- Search “Junior Developer salary in [City]”

Be realistic based on:
- City
- Company size
- Startup vs MNC
- Your skill level

---

# 💬 5️⃣ When HR Offers Lower Than Expected

### Professional Response

```
Thank you for the offer. I truly appreciate the opportunity.

Considering my skills and project experience, is there flexibility in the offered package?
```

Stay respectful — never emotional.

---

# 🤝 6️⃣ How to Negotiate Politely

Structure:

1. Thank them
2. Express excitement
3. Mention skills/value
4. Ask for revision

Example:

```
I am excited about the opportunity to join your team.

Based on my experience in full-stack development and project work, I was expecting something closer to ₹X.

Is there any possibility of revising the offer?
```

---

# ⚠️ 7️⃣ What NOT To Do

❌ Do not compare with friends  
❌ Do not say “Other company is giving more” aggressively  
❌ Do not sound desperate  
❌ Do not demand without justification  
❌ Do not negotiate before selection  

---

# 🧠 8️⃣ If You Have Multiple Offers

Professional Answer:

```
I do have another offer, but I am more interested in this role because of growth opportunities.

If possible, I would appreciate reconsideration of the compensation.
```

Keep tone calm and mature.

---

# 📅 9️⃣ Negotiating After Offer Letter

You can negotiate:

- Before signing
- Before joining
- During discussion call

Avoid negotiating after accepting formally unless necessary.

---

# 📈 🔟 Performance-Based Growth Strategy

If salary cannot increase now:

Ask:

```
Is there a performance review after 6 months?

What are the growth and increment policies?
```

Sometimes growth matters more than starting salary.

---

# 🏢 1️⃣1️⃣ Startup vs MNC Salary Strategy

### Startup
- Slightly flexible
- Negotiation possible
- Learning opportunity high

### MNC
- Fixed band
- Less flexibility
- Structured growth

Adjust your approach accordingly.

---

# 🧘 1️⃣2️⃣ Confidence Rules

✔ Speak slowly  
✔ Maintain eye contact  
✔ Do not over-explain  
✔ Be clear and concise  
✔ Smile and stay calm  

---

# 🎯 1️⃣3️⃣ Final Decision Factors (Not Just Salary)

Before accepting offer, consider:

- Learning opportunity
- Tech stack
- Work culture
- Work-life balance
- Growth path
- Stability
- Location

Salary is important — but growth is more important early in career.

---

# 🚀 Final Advice

For Freshers:

👉 First job = Learning + Experience  
👉 Second job = Salary jump  
👉 Third job = Strong positioning  

Build skills first. Money follows skills.
