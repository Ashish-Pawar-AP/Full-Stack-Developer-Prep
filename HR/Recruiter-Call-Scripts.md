# 📞 Recruiter Call Scripts Guide (All Scenarios)

---

# 📌 1️⃣ How to Answer a Recruiter Call

When you receive a call:

```
Hello, this is Ashish speaking.
```

If they introduce themselves:

```
Hello [Name], thank you for calling.
```

Speak clearly, confidently, and calmly.

---

# 🧑‍💼 2️⃣ Basic Recruiter Screening Questions

---

## ❓ Tell me about yourself.

Short version (30–45 seconds):

```
I am a BSc Computer Science graduate and a Junior Full Stack Developer skilled in React, Node.js, and MongoDB.

I have built full-stack projects and completed an internship as an Application Support Engineer.

I am currently looking for an opportunity where I can contribute and grow as a developer.
```

---

## ❓ What technologies do you work with?

```
I primarily work with React for frontend and Node.js with MongoDB for backend development.

I am also comfortable with REST APIs and basic deployment.
```

---

## ❓ What is your current location?

```
I am currently based in [Your Location].
```

---

## ❓ Are you open to relocation?

```
I am open to discussing relocation depending on the opportunity and growth prospects.
```

---

## ❓ What is your notice period?

```
I am available to join immediately.
```

---

# 💰 3️⃣ Salary Questions During Call

---

## ❓ What is your current CTC?

(Fresher)

```
As a fresher, I do not have a full-time CTC yet.
```

---

## ❓ What are your salary expectations?

```
As a fresher, my priority is learning and growth.

I am open to the company’s standard package for this role.
```

If they push for number:

```
Based on market standards, I believe ₹X–₹Y would be reasonable. However, I am open to discussion.
```

---

# 📅 4️⃣ Interview Scheduling

---

## ❓ Are you available for interview tomorrow?

```
Yes, I am available. Please let me know the time and mode of interview.
```

If not available:

```
I have a prior commitment tomorrow. Would it be possible to schedule it on [Alternative Date]?
```

---

# 📂 5️⃣ When Recruiter Asks to Send Resume

```
Sure, I will share my updated resume shortly.

Could you please confirm the email address?
```

---

# 🧠 6️⃣ When Recruiter Asks Tricky Questions

---

## ❓ Why are you looking for a job change?

```
I am looking for opportunities aligned with full stack development and long-term growth.
```

---

## ❓ Do you have other offers?

```
I am currently exploring opportunities, but I am particularly interested in roles that offer strong learning potential.
```

---

## ❓ Why should we consider you?

```
I am disciplined, quick to learn, and have practical project experience.

I am ready to contribute and grow within the organization.
```

---

# 🛑 7️⃣ If You Miss the Recruiter Call

Call back:

```
Hello, this is Ashish. I noticed a missed call from this number regarding a job opportunity.

I am returning your call.
```

Or message/email:

```
Hello, I missed your call earlier. Please let me know a convenient time to connect.
```

---

# ❌ 8️⃣ If Role Is Not Suitable

```
Thank you for considering me.

Currently, I am looking for roles focused on full stack development. I would appreciate it if you could keep me in mind for similar opportunities.
```

---

# ⏳ 9️⃣ If You Need Time to Think About Offer

```
Thank you for the offer.

May I have some time to review the details and get back to you?
```

---

# 🧘 🔟 Call Etiquette Rules

✔ Sit in quiet place  
✔ Keep resume open  
✔ Keep notebook ready  
✔ Speak slowly  
✔ Do not interrupt  
✔ Do not negotiate aggressively  
✔ Smile while speaking (improves tone)

---

# 🚀 1️⃣1️⃣ Ending the Call Professionally

```
Thank you for your time.

I look forward to the next steps.
Have a great day.
```

---

# 🎯 Final Advice

Recruiter call is:

- First impression
- Screening stage
- Short (5–15 minutes)
- Professional evaluation

Keep answers:
✔ Short  
✔ Clear  
✔ Confident  
✔ Positive
