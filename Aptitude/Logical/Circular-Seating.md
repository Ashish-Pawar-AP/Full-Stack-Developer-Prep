# 🔵 Logical Reasoning – Circular Seating Arrangement  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 5 Sets (Puzzle-Based Questions) with Answers.

---

## 📌 Important Rules

- In a circular arrangement facing center:
  - Right → Clockwise  
  - Left → Anti-clockwise  

- If facing outside:
  - Right → Anti-clockwise  
  - Left → Clockwise  

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (Set 1–5)

---

## ✅ Set 1

Eight persons A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H sit around a circular table facing center.

1. A is second to the right of B.  
2. C is opposite A.  
3. D is immediate left of C.  
4. E is opposite B.  
5. F is immediate right of A.  
6. G is between E and D.

**Answer Arrangement (Clockwise):**

B – A – F – C – D – G – E – H  

---

## ✅ Set 2

Six persons P, Q, R, S, T, U sit around facing center.

1. P sits opposite Q.  
2. R is immediate right of P.  
3. S sits between Q and T.  
4. U sits left of T.

**Answer (Clockwise):**

P – R – T – U – Q – S  

---

## ✅ Set 3

Five friends A, B, C, D, E sit in circle facing center.

1. A is right of B.  
2. C opposite A.  
3. D between B and C.

**Answer:**

B – A – E – C – D  

---

## ✅ Set 4

Seven persons seated facing center.

1. A opposite D.  
2. B left of A.  
3. C right of D.

**Answer Example:**

A – F – B – E – D – C – G  

---

## ✅ Set 5

Eight persons facing center.

1. M opposite N.  
2. O left of M.  
3. P right of N.

**Answer:**

M – O – Q – R – N – P – S – T
