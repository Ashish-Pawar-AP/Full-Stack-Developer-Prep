# 🧠 Logical Reasoning – Statement & Argument  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each question contains a **Statement** followed by **Arguments I & II**.  
Choose the correct option:

1️⃣ Only Argument I is strong  
2️⃣ Only Argument II is strong  
3️⃣ Both I and II are strong  
4️⃣ Neither I nor II is strong
