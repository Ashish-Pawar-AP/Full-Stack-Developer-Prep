# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (Set 1–2)

---

## ✅ Set 1 – Linear Seating (5 Persons)

Five friends A, B, C, D, E sit in a row facing North.

1. A sits to the immediate left of B.  
2. C sits at the extreme right.  
3. D sits between A and C.  
4. E sits at the extreme left.

**Final Arrangement (Left → Right):**  
E – A – B – D – C  

---

## ✅ Set 2 – Order Puzzle (5 Students)

Five students P, Q, R, S, T scored different marks.

1. P scored more than Q but less than R.  
2. T scored less than Q.  
3. S scored highest.

**Final Order (Highest → Lowest):**  
S – R – P – Q – T

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (Set 3–4)

---

## ✅ Set 3 – Circular Seating (6 Persons)

Six persons A, B, C, D, E, F sit around a table facing center.

1. A sits opposite C.  
2. B sits to immediate right of A.  
3. D sits between B and E.  
4. F sits opposite B.

**Final Clockwise Arrangement:**  
A – B – D – E – C – F  

---

## ✅ Set 4 – Floor Puzzle (5 Floors)

Five persons A–E live on different floors (1–5).

1. A lives above C.  
2. B lives below D.  
3. E lives on top floor.  
4. C does not live on 1st floor.

**Final Arrangement (Top → Bottom):**  
5 – E  
4 – A  
3 – D  
2 – C  
1 – B

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL (Set 5–6)

## ✅ Set 5 – Box Puzzle

Seven boxes A–G kept one above another.

1. A above B but below C.  
2. D above F.  
3. G at bottom.  
4. E between C and D.  
5. B above G.

**Final Arrangement (Top → Bottom):**  
C – E – A – B – D – F – G  

---

## ✅ Set 6 – Month & Person Puzzle

Five persons A–E born in Jan–May.

1. A born before C but after B.  
2. D born in May.  
3. E born in February.  
4. C not born in April.

**Final Order:**  
Jan – B  
Feb – E  
Mar – A  
Apr – C  
May – D

## ✅ Set 7 – Mixed Seating (8 Persons)

Eight persons A–H sit in a row facing North.

1. A is 3rd from left.  
2. B sits immediate right of D.  
3. C sits between A and E.  
4. F sits at extreme right.  
5. G not adjacent to A.  
6. H sits 2nd from left.

**Final Arrangement:**  
X – H – A – C – E – D – B – F  

---

## ✅ Set 8 – Double Row Puzzle

Six persons A–F sit in two rows facing each other (3 each).

Row 1: A, B, C  
Row 2: D, E, F  

1. A sits opposite D.  
2. B not opposite F.  
3. C sits opposite E.  
4. F sits opposite B.

**Final Arrangement:**

Row 1 (North): A – B – C  
Row 2 (South): D – F – E
