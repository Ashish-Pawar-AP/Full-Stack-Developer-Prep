# 🧠 Logical Reasoning – Syllogism  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)

For each question, choose:

1️⃣ Only Conclusion I follows  
2️⃣ Only Conclusion II follows  
3️⃣ Both I and II follow  
4️⃣ Neither I nor II follows  

---
