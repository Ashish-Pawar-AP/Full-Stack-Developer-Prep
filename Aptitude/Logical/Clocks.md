## 📌 Important Formulae

- Angle between hands  
  = |30H − 5.5M|  

- When hands coincide → Every 65 5/11 minutes  

- When hands are opposite → Every 65 5/11 minutes (but shifted)

- In 12 hours →  
  - Coincide = 11 times  
  - Right angle = 22 times
