# 🪑 Logical Reasoning – Seating Arrangement  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Includes Linear, Circular, Double Row & Mixed Arrangement Sets.

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (Set 1–2)

---

## ✅ Set 1 – Linear (Facing North)

Five persons A, B, C, D, E sit in a row.

1. A is immediate left of B.  
2. C is at extreme right.  
3. D sits between B and C.  
4. E sits at extreme left.

**Final Arrangement (Left → Right):**  
E – A – B – D – C  

---

## ✅ Set 2 – Circular (Facing Center)

Six persons P, Q, R, S, T, U sit around a table.

1. P sits opposite R.  
2. Q sits immediate right of P.  
3. S sits between Q and T.  
4. U sits opposite Q.

**Final Clockwise Arrangement:**  
P – Q – S – T – R – U  

---

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (Set 3–4)

---

## ✅ Set 3 – Linear (Facing South)

Seven persons A–G sit in a row facing South.

1. A is second from right.  
2. B is immediate left of A.  
3. C is at extreme left.  
4. D sits between C and B.  
5. E is not adjacent to A.

**Final Arrangement (Left → Right):**  
C – D – B – A – F – E – G  

---

## ✅ Set 4 – Double Row (Facing Each Other)

Six persons A, B, C sit in Row 1 (North).  
D, E, F sit in Row 2 (South).

1. A sits opposite D.  
2. B not opposite F.  
3. C sits opposite E.  
4. F sits opposite B.

**Final Arrangement:**

Row 1 (North): A – B – C  
Row 2 (South): D – F – E

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL (Set 5–6)

## ✅ Set 5 – Circular (8 Persons Facing Center)

A, B, C, D, E, F, G, H sit around a circle.

1. A sits opposite D.  
2. B sits second to the right of A.  
3. C sits between B and E.  
4. F sits immediate left of D.  
5. G not adjacent to A.

**Final Clockwise Arrangement:**  
A – X – B – C – E – D – F – G  

---

## ✅ Set 6 – Linear with Conditions

Nine persons A–I sit in a row facing North.

1. A sits fourth from left.  
2. B sits third to the right of A.  
3. C sits between A and D.  
4. E sits immediate left of C.  
5. I sits at extreme right.  
6. F not adjacent to B.

**Final Arrangement:**  
G – H – E – C – A – D – B – F – I

# 🔴 VERY HARD LEVEL (Set 7–8)

---

## ✅ Set 7 – Mixed Facing Circular

Eight persons A–H sit around a circular table.  
Some face center, some face outside.

1. A faces center and sits opposite C.  
2. B sits immediate right of A.  
3. D sits opposite E.  
4. F sits between B and H.  
5. G not adjacent to C.

**Final Example Arrangement (Clockwise):**  
A – B – F – H – C – D – E – G  

---

## ✅ Set 8 – Floor + Seating Combined

Eight persons A–H live on 8 floors (1–8).

1. A lives above C but below D.  
2. B lives on top floor.  
3. E lives on 3rd floor.  
4. F lives immediately below D.  
5. H lives at bottom.  
6. G lives above A.

**Final Floor Arrangement (Top → Bottom):**

8 – B  
7 – D  
6 – F  
5 – G  
4 – A  
3 – E  
2 – C  
1 – H
