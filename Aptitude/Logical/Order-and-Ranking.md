# 🏆 Logical Reasoning – Order & Ranking  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 10 Questions with Answers.

---

## 📌 Important Concepts

- Total persons = Left rank + Right rank − 1  
- If position from top & bottom given:  
  Total = Top rank + Bottom rank − 1  
- Reverse position = Total − Given rank + 1  

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
A is 5th from top in a class. What is his position from top?

**Answer:**  
5th  

---

## Q2.
B is 3rd from bottom in a row of 10. What is his rank from top?

**Answer:**  
10 − 3 + 1 = 8th  

---

## Q3.

**Answer:**
