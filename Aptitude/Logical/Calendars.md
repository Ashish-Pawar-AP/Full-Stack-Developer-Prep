## 📌 Important Concepts

- Ordinary Year = 365 days → 1 odd day  
- Leap Year = 366 days → 2 odd days  
- Leap year rule:
  - Divisible by 4 ✔  
  - Century year must be divisible by 400 ✔  

- Total odd days = (Total days ÷ 7 remainder)  
- Week repeats every 7 days

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (11–20)

## Q11.
If today is Wednesday, what day after 20 days?

**Answer:**  
20 ÷ 7 = remainder 6  

Wednesday + 6 = Tuesday  

---

## Q12.
How many odd days in 100 years?

**Answer:**  
100 years → 24 leap years  

Odd days = 76 + 48  
= 124  

124 ÷ 7 remainder = 5  

Answer: 5 odd days  

---

## Q13.
