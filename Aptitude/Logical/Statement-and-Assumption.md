# 🧠 Logical Reasoning – Statement & Assumption  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  

For each question:  
Choose the correct option:

1️⃣ Only Assumption I is implicit  
2️⃣ Only Assumption II is implicit  
3️⃣ Both I and II are implicit  
4️⃣ Neither I nor II is implicit
