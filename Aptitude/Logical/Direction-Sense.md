# 🧭 Logical Reasoning – Direction Sense  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 10 Questions with Answers.

---

## 📌 Important Concepts

- North (↑), South (↓), East (→), West (←)  
- Right turn = 90° clockwise  
- Left turn = 90° anti-clockwise  
- Opposite directions:
  - North ↔ South  
  - East ↔ West  

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
A man walks 10 km towards North. In which direction is he moving?

**Answer:**  
North  

---

## Q2.
A person faces East. Which direction is to his left?

**Answer:**  
North  

---

## Q3.
If you face South, which direction is on your right?

**Answer:**  
West  

---

## Q4.
A man walks 5 km North and then 5 km South. Where is he?

**Answer:**  
Starting point  

---

## Q5.
If facing West, what is opposite direction?

**Answer:**  
East  

---

## Q6.
A walks 10 km East. Then 10 km West. Final position?

**Answer:**  
Starting point  

---

## Q7.
Which direction is opposite to North?

**Answer:**  
South  

---

## Q8.
If facing North, which direction is right?

**Answer:**  
East  

---

## Q9.
A person walks 5 km South. Which direction is he going?

**Answer:**  
South  

---

## Q10.
If you turn left from North, which direction?

**Answer:**  
West

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (11–20)

## Q11.

**Answer:**

---

## Q12.
A faces East. Turns right, then left. Final direction?

**Answer:**  
South  

---

South-West

## Q15.
If facing South and turn left twice, final direction?

√(100+100)
