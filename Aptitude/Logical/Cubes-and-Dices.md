# 🎲 Logical Reasoning – Cubes & Dice  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 10 Questions with Answers.

---

## 📌 Important Concepts

- A cube has:
  - 6 Faces  
  - 12 Edges  
  - 8 Corners  

- Opposite faces:
  - Never appear together  
- Adjacent faces:
  - Share a common edge  

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
How many faces does a cube have?

**Answer:**  
6  

---

## Q2.
How many edges does a cube have?

**Answer:**  
12  

---

## Q3.
How many corners does a cube have?

**Answer:**  
8  

---

## Q4.
If a dice shows 1 opposite 6, what is opposite of 6?

**Answer:**  
1  

---

## Q5.
If 2 is opposite 5, which faces are adjacent to 2?

**Answer:**  
All except 5  

---

## Q6.
If a cube has 1,2,3 on adjacent faces, can 1 and 3 be opposite?

**Answer:**  
No  

---

## Q7.
In a standard dice, what is sum of opposite faces?

**Answer:**  
7  

---

## Q8.
If 4 is opposite 3, what is opposite of 4?

**Answer:**  
3  

---

## Q9.
If 1,2,3 meet at a corner, which cannot be opposite to 1?

**Answer:**  
2 or 3  

---

## Q10.
How many pairs of opposite faces in a cube?

**Answer:**  
3 pairs
