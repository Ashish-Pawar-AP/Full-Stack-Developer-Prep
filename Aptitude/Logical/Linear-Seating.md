# 🪑 Logical Reasoning – Linear Seating Arrangement  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains puzzle-based Sets with final arrangements.

---

## 📌 Important Rules

- Left / Right is based on direction person is facing.  
- If facing North →  
  - Left = Your left  
  - Right = Your right  
- If facing South →  
  - Left = Opposite direction  

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (Set 1–3)

---

## ✅ Set 1 (All Facing North)

Five persons A, B, C, D, E sit in a row.

1. A is to the left of B.  
2. C is to the right of B.  
3. D is at extreme left.  
4. E is at extreme right.

**Final Arrangement (Left → Right):**  
D – A – B – C – E  

---

## ✅ Set 2 (All Facing North)

Six persons P, Q, R, S, T, U.

1. P is between Q and R.  
2. S is to the immediate right of R.  
3. T is at extreme left.  
4. U is immediate left of Q.

**Final Arrangement:**  
T – U – Q – P – R – S  

---

## ✅ Set 3 (All Facing North)

Seven persons A–G.

1. A sits third from left.  
2. B is to immediate left of A.  
3. C at extreme right.  
4. D between B and E.  

**Final Arrangement:**  
F – B – A – D – E – G – C  

---

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (Set 4–6)

---

## ✅ Set 4 (All Facing South)

Eight persons A–H.

1. A is second from right.  
2. B is immediate left of A.  
3. C at extreme left.  
4. D between C and B.  
5. E is third from left.

**Final Arrangement (Left → Right):**  
C – D – B – A – E – F – G – H  

---

## ✅ Set 5 (Mixed Information)

Six persons A–F facing North.

1. A is not at extreme end.  
2. B is second to the left of C.  
3. D sits immediate right of A.  
4. F is at extreme right.  
5. E between C and A.

**Final Arrangement:**  
C – E – A – D – B – F  

---

## ✅ Set 6 (All Facing North)

Seven persons.

1. P is third from right.  
2. Q is immediate left of P.  
3. R is second from left.  
4. S between R and Q.

**Final Arrangement:**  
X – R – S – Q – P – Y – Z

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL (Set 7–8)

---

## ✅ Set 7 (Mixed Facing)

Eight persons A–H.

1. A faces North, B faces South.  
2. C sits immediate right of A.  
3. D opposite C (in row facing opposite).  
4. E at extreme left.  
5. F between B and H.

**Final Example Arrangement:**  
E – A – C – G – D – H – B – F  

---

## ✅ Set 8 (All Facing North)

Nine persons A–I.

1. A sits fourth from left.  
2. B is third to the right of A.  
3. C between A and D.  
4. E immediate left of C.  
5. I at extreme right.

**Final Arrangement:**  
F – G – E – C – A – D – B – H – I  

---

# 🔴 VERY HARD LEVEL (Set 9–10)

---

## ✅ Set 9 (Variable Gaps)

Ten persons A–J.

1. A second from left.  
2. B fourth from right.  
3. C between A and D.  
4. E immediate right of B.  
5. F at extreme right.  
6. G between C and E.

**Final Example:**  
X – A – C – D – G – B – E – H – I – F  

---

## ✅ Set 10 (Complex Conditions)

Eight persons A–H.

1. A not adjacent to B.  
2. C at extreme left.  
3. D immediate right of E.  
4. F second from right.  
5. G between A and H.  
6. B immediate left of F.

**Final Example Arrangement:**  
C – H – G – A – E – D – B – F
