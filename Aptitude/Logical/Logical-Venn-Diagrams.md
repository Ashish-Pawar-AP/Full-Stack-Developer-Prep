# 🔵 Logical Reasoning – Venn Diagrams  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 10 Questions with Answers.

---

## 📌 Basic Concepts

- **All A are B** → A circle inside B  
- **Some A are B** → Partial overlap  
- **No A is B** → Separate circles  
- **Some A are not B** → Part of A outside B
