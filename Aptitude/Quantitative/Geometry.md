# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
Find the perimeter of a rectangle with length = 10 cm and breadth = 6 cm.

**Answer:**  
Perimeter = 2(l + b)  
= 2(10 + 6)  
= 32 cm  

---

## Q2.
Find area of a square of side 8 cm.

**Answer:**  
Area = side²  
= 8² = 64 cm²  

---

## Q3.
Find circumference of a circle of radius 7 cm. (π = 22/7)

**Answer:**  
Circumference = 2πr  
= 2 × 22/7 × 7  
= 44 cm  

---

## Q4.
Find area of a circle of radius 7 cm.

**Answer:**  
Area = πr²  
= 22/7 × 49  
= 154 cm²  

---

## Q5.
Find area of a triangle with base = 10 cm and height = 6 cm.

**Answer:**  
Area = ½ × base × height  
= ½ × 10 × 6  
= 30 cm²  

---

## Q6.
Find sum of interior angles of a triangle.

**Answer:**  
180°  

---

## Q7.
Find perimeter of square with side 12 cm.

**Answer:**  
4 × 12 = 48 cm  

---

## Q8.
Find diagonal of square of side 5 cm.

**Answer:**  
Diagonal = a√2  
= 5√2 cm  

---

## Q9.
Find area of rectangle 15 cm × 4 cm.

**Answer:**  
Area = 60 cm²  

---

## Q10.
If one angle of triangle is 90° and another is 30°, find third.

**Answer:**  
180 − 120 = 60°

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (11–20)

## Q11.
Find area of right triangle with legs 9 cm and 12 cm.

**Answer:**  
Area = ½ × 9 × 12  
= 54 cm²  

---

## Q12.
Find hypotenuse of right triangle with sides 6 cm and 8 cm.

**Answer:**  
c² = 36 + 64 = 100  
c = 10 cm  

---

## Q13.
Find area of trapezium with parallel sides 10 cm, 6 cm and height 5 cm.

**Answer:**  
Area = ½ × (10 + 6) × 5  
= 40 cm²  

---

## Q14.
Find volume of cube of side 5 cm.

**Answer:**  
Volume = a³  
= 125 cm³  

---

## Q15.
Find surface area of cube of side 4 cm.

**Answer:**  
Surface area = 6a²  
= 6 × 16  
= 96 cm²  

---

## Q16.
Find volume of cylinder with r = 7 cm, h = 10 cm.

**Answer:**  
Volume = πr²h  
= 22/7 × 49 × 10  
= 1540 cm³  

---

## Q17.
Find area of semicircle radius 14 cm.

**Answer:**  
Area = ½ πr²  
= ½ × 22/7 × 196  
= 308 cm²  

---

## Q18.
Find sum of interior angles of pentagon.

**Answer:**  
(n − 2) × 180  
= 3 × 180  
= 540°  

---

## Q19.
Find area of rhombus with diagonals 10 cm and 8 cm.

**Answer:**  
Area = ½ × d₁ × d₂  
= ½ × 10 × 8  
= 40 cm²  

---

## Q20.
Find lateral surface area of cylinder (r=7 cm, h=5 cm).

**Answer:**  
2πrh  
= 2 × 22/7 × 7 × 5  
= 220 cm²

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL (21–30)

## Q21.
Find diagonal of rectangle 9 cm × 12 cm.

**Answer:**  
d² = 81 + 144  
= 225  
d = 15 cm  

---

## Q22.

**Answer:**

# 🔴 VERY HARD LEVEL (31–40)

## Q31.
Find area of triangle with sides 5, 6, 7 cm.

**Answer:**  
Using Heron’s formula:  

s = 9  
Area = √[9(4)(3)(2)]  
= √216  
= 14.7 cm² approx  

---

## Q32.
Find height of equilateral triangle side 8 cm.

**Answer:**  
Height = √3/2 × 8  
= 4√3 cm  

---

## Q33.
Find diagonal of cube side 6 cm.

**Answer:**  
Diagonal = a√3  
= 6√3 cm  

---

## Q34.
Find curved surface area of sphere r=7 cm.

**Answer:**  
4πr²  
= 4 × 22/7 × 49  
= 616 cm²  

---

## Q35.
Find volume of cone r=7 cm, h=12 cm.

**Answer:**  
1/3 πr²h  
= 1/3 × 22/7 × 49 × 12  
= 616 cm³  

---

## Q36.
Find area of ring with outer radius 10 cm and inner radius 7 cm.

**Answer:**  
π(R² − r²)  
= 22/7 (100 − 49)  
= 160.3 cm² approx  

---

## Q37.
Find angle of sector if area = 77 cm² and r = 7 cm.

**Answer:**  
(θ/360)πr² = 77  
θ = 90°  

---

## Q38.
Find surface area of cylinder r=5 cm, h=10 cm.

**Answer:**  
2πr(r + h)  
= 2 × 3.14 × 5 × 15  
= 471 cm² approx  

---

## Q39.
Find side of equilateral triangle if area = 16√3 cm².

**Answer:**  
√3/4 × a² = 16√3  
a² = 64  
a = 8 cm  

---

## Q40.
Find distance between opposite vertices of rectangle 8 cm × 15 cm.

**Answer:**  
d² = 64 + 225  
= 289  
d = 17 cm
