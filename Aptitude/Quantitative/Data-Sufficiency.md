# 📘 Data Sufficiency – Interview Aptitude Practice Set  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 10 Questions with Answers.  

---

## 📌 Directions (For All Questions)

Each question is followed by two statements (I) and (II).  
Choose the correct option:

A) Statement I alone is sufficient  
B) Statement II alone is sufficient  
C) Both I and II together are sufficient  
D) Either I or II alone is sufficient  
E) Data insufficient even after both statements
