# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
Speed of boat in still water = 10 km/hr  
Speed of stream = 2 km/hr  
Find downstream speed.

**Answer:**  
Downstream = 10 + 2 = 12 km/hr  

---

## Q2.
Speed of boat in still water = 15 km/hr  
Stream speed = 3 km/hr  
Find upstream speed.

**Answer:**  
Upstream = 15 − 3 = 12 km/hr  

---

## Q3.
Boat downstream speed = 20 km/hr  
Upstream speed = 14 km/hr  
Find speed in still water.

**Answer:**  
Still water speed = (20 + 14) / 2  
= 17 km/hr

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL (21–30)

## Q21.
Boat speed in still water = 24 km/hr  
Stream speed = 6 km/hr  
Total time for 120 km downstream and back?

**Answer:**  
Downstream = 30 km/hr  
Upstream = 18 km/hr  

Time = (120/30) + (120/18)  
= 4 + 6.67  
= 10.67 hrs  

---

## Q22.
Boat takes 4 hrs downstream and 6 hrs upstream for same distance. Find ratio of still water and stream speeds.

**Answer:**  
Speed ratio = 5 : 1  

---

## Q23.
Difference between downstream & upstream speeds = 10 km/hr  
Still water speed = 25 km/hr  
Find stream speed.

**Answer:**  
Stream speed = 5 km/hr  

---

## Q24.
Boat covers 150 km downstream in 5 hrs and upstream in 10 hrs. Find stream speed.

**Answer:**  
Downstream = 30  
Upstream = 15  

Stream = (30 − 15)/2  
= 7.5 km/hr  

---

## Q25.
If boat speed doubled, upstream speed becomes 20 km/hr. Stream speed = 4 km/hr. Find original boat speed.

**Answer:**  
2x − 4 = 20  
2x = 24  
x = 12 km/hr  

---

## Q26.
Boat travels 100 km upstream and 100 km downstream in 10 hrs. Still water speed = 25 km/hr. Find stream speed.

**Answer:**  
Let stream speed = x  

(100/(25+x)) + (100/(25−x)) = 10  

x = 5 km/hr

## Q30.
