## 📌 Important Formula (Alligation)

If two items at price P₁ and P₂ are mixed to get mean price M:

Ratio = (P₂ − M) : (M − P₁)

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
In what ratio should rice at ₹40/kg be mixed with rice at ₹60/kg to get ₹50/kg?

**Answer:**  
60 − 50 : 50 − 40  
10 : 10  
= 1 : 1
