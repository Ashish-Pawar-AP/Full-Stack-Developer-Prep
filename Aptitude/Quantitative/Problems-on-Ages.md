# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
The sum of ages of A and B is 40. A is 6 years older than B. Find their ages.

**Answer:**  
Let B = x  
A = x + 6  

x + (x + 6) = 40  
2x = 34  
x = 17  

B = 17  
A = 23

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (11–20)

## Q11.
A father is 3 times his son’s age. After 10 years, he will be twice. Find present ages.

**Answer:**  
Let son = x  
Father = 3x  

3x + 10 = 2(x + 10)  
3x + 10 = 2x + 20  
x = 10  

Son = 10  
Father = 30  

---

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL (21–30)

## Q21.
A is twice B. 5 years ago A was thrice B. Find present ages.

**Answer:**  
Let B = x  
A = 2x  

2x − 5 = 3(x − 5)  
2x − 5 = 3x − 15  
x = 10  

B = 10  
A = 20  

---

## Q22.
A father is 5 times son. After 20 years, father will be twice. Find present ages.

**Answer:**  
Let son = x  
Father = 5x  

5x + 20 = 2(x + 20)  
5x + 20 = 2x + 40  
3x = 20  
x = 20/3  

Son = 6.67  
Father = 33.33

# 🔴 VERY HARD LEVEL (31–40)

## Q31.
A father is 6 times his son. After 5 years, father will be 4 times. Find present ages.

**Answer:**  
6x + 5 = 4(x + 5)  
6x + 5 = 4x + 20  
2x = 15  
x = 7.5  

Son = 7.5  
Father = 45  

---

## Q32.
The sum of ages of A and B is 50. 5 years ago, ratio was 4:5. Find ages.

**Answer:**  
Let ages = x and 50 − x  

(x − 5)/(45 − x) = 4/5  
x = 25  

Ages = 25 and 25  

---

## Q33.
A is 3 times B. 10 years ago A was 5 times B. Find present ages.

**Answer:**  
3x − 10 = 5(x − 10)  
3x − 10 = 5x − 50  
2x = 40  
x = 20  

B = 20  
A = 60  

---

## Q34.
The average age of family of 4 is 30. If father is 40, mother 35, find sum of children ages.

**Answer:**  
Total = 120  
Children = 120 − 75  
= 45  

---

## Q35.
Ratio of A and B is 3:4. After 5 years ratio becomes 4:5. Find ages.

**Answer:**  
3x + 5 / 4x + 5 = 4/5  
x = 5  

A = 15  
B = 20  

---

## Q36.
A is 5 years younger than B. 5 years ago, B was twice A. Find ages.

**Answer:**  
x − 5 − 5 = 2(x − 5)  
x − 10 = 2x − 10  
x = 0  

B = 0  
A = −5 (Not realistic)  

---

## Q37.
The sum of ages of father and son is 60. 10 years ago father was 5 times son. Find ages.

**Answer:**  
x + (60 − x) = 60  

(60 − x − 10) = 5(x − 10)  
50 − x = 5x − 50  
6x = 100  
x = 16.67  

Son = 16.67  
Father = 43.33  

---

## Q38.
A is twice B. C is 5 years younger than B. Sum of ages is 45. Find ages.

**Answer:**  
2x + x + (x − 5) = 45  
4x − 5 = 45  
x = 12.5  

B = 12.5  
A = 25  
C = 7.5  

---

## Q39.
