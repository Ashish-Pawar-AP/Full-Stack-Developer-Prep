## 📌 Important Formulae

- Speed = Distance / Time  
- Distance = Speed × Time  
- Time = Distance / Speed  
- 1 km/hr = 5/18 m/s  
- Relative Speed (Opposite) = S₁ + S₂  
- Relative Speed (Same Direction) = |S₁ − S₂|

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
If speed = 60 km/hr, time = 2 hours. Find distance.

**Answer:**  
Distance = 60 × 2  
= 120 km

## Q12.
A car covers 300 km at 60 km/hr and returns at 100 km/hr. Find average speed.

**Answer:**  
Average speed = (2xy)/(x+y)  

= (2×60×100)/(160)  
= 75 km/hr

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL (21–30)

## Q21.
A car covers first half distance at 40 km/hr and second half at 60 km/hr. Average speed?

**Answer:**  
(2×40×60)/(100)  
= 48 km/hr  

---

## Q22.
A man travels 100 km partly at 50 km/hr and partly at 25 km/hr. Total time 3 hrs. Find distance at 50 km/hr.

**Answer:**  
Let x  

x/50 + (100−x)/25 = 3  

x = 50 km  

---

## Q31.
A boat travels 30 km upstream in 5 hrs and 30 km downstream in 3 hrs. Find speed in still water.

**Answer:**

Downstream = 10 km/hr  

Still water = (6+10)/2  
= 8 km/hr
