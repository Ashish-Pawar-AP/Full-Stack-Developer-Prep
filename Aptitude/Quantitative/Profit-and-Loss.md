# 💰 Profit & Loss – Interview Aptitude Practice Set  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 10 Questions with Answers.

---

## 📌 Important Formulae

- Profit = SP − CP  
- Loss = CP − SP  
- Profit% = (Profit / CP) × 100  
- Loss% = (Loss / CP) × 100  
- SP = CP(1 + Profit%)  
- SP = CP(1 − Loss%)  

---
