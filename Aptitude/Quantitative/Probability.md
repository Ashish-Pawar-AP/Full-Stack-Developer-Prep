## 📌 Important Formulae

- Probability (P) = Favorable outcomes / Total outcomes  
- 0 ≤ P ≤ 1  
- P(A') = 1 − P(A)  
- For independent events → P(A ∩ B) = P(A) × P(B)  
- nCr used for combinations
