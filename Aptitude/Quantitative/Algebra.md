# 🧮 Aptitude Algebra Questions (Easy → Very Hard)

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL

## Q1.
If 4x + 8 = 24, find x.

**Answer:**
4x = 24 − 8  
4x = 16  
x = 4  

---

## Q2.
If x/5 = 7, find x.

**Answer:**
x = 7 × 5  
x = 35  

---

## Q3.
Solve: x² − 9 = 0

**Answer:**
x² = 9  
x = ±3  

---

## Q4.
The sum of two numbers is 30. One number is 12. Find the other.

**Answer:**
Other number = 30 − 12  
= 18  

---

## Q5.
Simplify: (x + 2)(x + 5)

**Answer:**
= x² + 5x + 2x + 10  
= x² + 7x + 10

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL

## Q6.
Solve:
2x + 3 = 15

**Answer:**
2x = 12  
x = 6  

---

## Q7.
Solve:
x² − 7x + 10 = 0

**Answer:**
(x − 5)(x − 2) = 0  
x = 5 or x = 2  

---

## Q8.
The ratio of two numbers is 4:7 and their sum is 55. Find the numbers.

**Answer:**
Let numbers be 4x and 7x  

4x + 7x = 55  
11x = 55  
x = 5  

Numbers = 20 and 35  

---

## Q9.
A number increased by 25% becomes 50. Find the number.

**Answer:**
Let number = x  

x + 0.25x = 50  
1.25x = 50  
x = 40  

---

## Q10.
The product of two consecutive numbers is 132. Find them.

**Answer:**
Let first number = x  

x(x + 1) = 132  
x² + x − 132 = 0  

(x + 12)(x − 11) = 0  

x = 11 or −12  

Numbers = 11 & 12

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL

## Q11.
Solve:
3x² − 5x − 2 = 0

**Answer:**
Using quadratic formula:

x = [5 ± √(25 + 24)] / 6  
x = [5 ± √49] / 6  
x = (5 ± 7)/6  

x = 2 or x = −1/3  

---

## Q12.
If x + 1/x = 5, find x² + 1/x².

**Answer:**
(x + 1/x)² = x² + 1/x² + 2  

5² = x² + 1/x² + 2  

25 = x² + 1/x² + 2  

x² + 1/x² = 23  

---

## Q13.
A father is 3 times as old as his son. After 10 years, he will be twice as old. Find present ages.

**Answer:**
Let son’s age = x  
Father’s age = 3x  

After 10 years:  

3x + 10 = 2(x + 10)  

3x + 10 = 2x + 20  

x = 10  

Son = 10 years  
Father = 30 years  

---

## Q14.
If a + b = 8 and ab = 12, find a² + b².

**Answer:**
a² + b² = (a + b)² − 2ab  

= 8² − 2(12)  
= 64 − 24  
= 40  

---

## Q15.
Solve:
x⁴ − 16 = 0

**Answer:**
x⁴ = 16  

x² = 4  

x = ±2

# 🔴 VERY HARD LEVEL

## Q16.
If x + y = 10 and xy = 21, find x³ + y³.

**Answer:**
x³ + y³ = (x + y)³ − 3xy(x + y)  

= 10³ − 3(21)(10)  
= 1000 − 630  
= 370  

---

## Q17.
Solve:
2x² − 4x + 1 = 0

**Answer:**
x = [4 ± √(16 − 8)] / 4  
x = [4 ± √8] / 4  
x = [4 ± 2√2] / 4  

x = (2 ± √2)/2  

---

## Q18.
If x − 1/x = 4, find x² + 1/x².

**Answer:**
(x − 1/x)² = x² + 1/x² − 2  

4² = x² + 1/x² − 2  

16 = x² + 1/x² − 2  

x² + 1/x² = 18  

---

## Q19.
Solve:
x³ − 6x² + 11x − 6 = 0

**Answer:**
(x − 1)(x − 2)(x − 3) = 0  

x = 1, 2, 3  

---

## Q20.
The difference of two numbers is 5 and their product is 84. Find the numbers.

**Answer:**
Let numbers be x and x − 5  

x(x − 5) = 84  

x² − 5x − 84 = 0  

(x − 12)(x + 7) = 0  

x = 12 or −7  

Numbers = 12 & 7
