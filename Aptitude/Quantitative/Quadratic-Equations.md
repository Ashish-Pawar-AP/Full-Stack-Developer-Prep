## 📌 Important Formulae

- Standard form → ax² + bx + c = 0  
- Roots formula → x = [-b ± √(b² − 4ac)] / 2a  
- Sum of roots = −b/a  
- Product of roots = c/a  
- Nature of roots depends on D = b² − 4ac
