## 📌 Important Concepts

- HCF × LCM = Product of two numbers  
- HCF → Greatest common divisor  
- LCM → Smallest common multiple

## Q23.
Two numbers have HCF 12 and LCM 180. If one number is 36, find other.

**Answer:**  
Other = (HCF × LCM) / 36  
= (12 × 180)/36  
= 60
