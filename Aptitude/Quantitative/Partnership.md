## 📌 Important Concept

Profit Sharing Ratio = (Investment × Time)

If  
A invests ₹x for t₁ months  
B invests ₹y for t₂ months  

Then ratio = x·t₁ : y·t₂  

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
A invests ₹10,000 for 1 year.  
B invests ₹20,000 for 1 year.  
Profit = ₹6,000. Find A’s share.

**Answer:**  
Ratio = 10000 : 20000  
= 1 : 2  

A’s share = 6000 × 1/3  
= ₹2000
