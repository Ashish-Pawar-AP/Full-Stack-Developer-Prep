## 📌 Important Formulae

- Mean (Average) = Sum of observations / Number of observations  
- Median (Odd n) = (n+1)/2 th term  
- Mode = Most frequent value  
- Range = Highest − Lowest  
- Variance (Basic) = Σ(x − mean)² / n
