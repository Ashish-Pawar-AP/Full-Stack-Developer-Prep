# ⏳ Time & Work – Interview Aptitude Practice Set  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 10 Questions with Answers.

---

## 📌 Important Formulae

- If A can do work in x days → 1 day work = 1/x  
- If A & B work together → 1/x + 1/y  
- Work = Rate × Time  
- Efficiency ∝ 1 / Time  
- If A is twice as efficient as B → A : B = 2 : 1

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
A can complete a work in 10 days. How much work in 1 day?

**Answer:**  
1/10  

---

**Answer:**

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (11–20)

A completes in 12 days. B in 18 days. Together?

**Answer:**

= 5/36

= 7.2 days  

---

## Q13.
A in 8 days. B in 12 days. Together?

**Answer:**

---

## Q14.
A does work in 6 days. After 2 days B joins. Together finish in 2 more days. Find B’s time.

**Answer:**  
A’s 2 days work = 2/6 = 1/3  

Remaining = 2/3  

(A+B) rate = (2/3)/2  
= 1/3  

B rate = 1/3 − 1/6  
= 1/6  

Time = 6 days

= 1/5

## Q22.

**Answer:**  
Ratio 2:1  

Total = 3 units  

1 unit = 36  

A = 18 days

## Q23.
A in 8 days. B in 16 days. A works 2 days alone then B joins. Total time?

**Answer:**  
A 2 days = 1/4  

Remaining = 3/4  

Together rate = 3/16  

Time = (3/4)/(3/16)  
= 4 days  

Total = 6 days
