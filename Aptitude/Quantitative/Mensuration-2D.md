## 📌 Important Formulae

- Rectangle → Area = l × b, Perimeter = 2(l + b)  
- Square → Area = a², Perimeter = 4a  
- Triangle → Area = ½ × b × h  
- Circle → Area = πr², Circumference = 2πr  
- Trapezium → Area = ½ (a + b)h  
- Rhombus → Area = ½ d₁d₂  
(Use π = 22/7 unless mentioned)

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
Find area of rectangle 12 cm × 8 cm.

**Answer:**  
Area = 12 × 8 = 96 cm²  

---

## Q2.
Find perimeter of square of side 9 cm.

**Answer:**

## Q22.

**Answer:**
