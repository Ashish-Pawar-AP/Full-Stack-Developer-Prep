# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
Find CI on ₹1000 at 10% per annum for 2 years.

**Answer:**  
Amount = 1000(1.1)²  
= 1000 × 1.21 = 1210  
CI = 1210 − 1000 = ₹210
