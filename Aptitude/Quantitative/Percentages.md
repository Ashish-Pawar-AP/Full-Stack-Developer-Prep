# 📊 Percentages – Interview Aptitude Practice Set  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 10 Questions with Answers.

---

## 📌 Important Concepts

- x% = x/100  
- % Increase = (Increase / Original) × 100  
- Successive % formula → Net % = a + b + (ab/100)  
- If price increases by x%, consumption must decrease by x/(100+x)

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
Find 20% of 250.

**Answer:**  
20/100 × 250 = 50  

---

## Q2.

**Answer:**  
0.15 × 400 = 60

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (11–20)

## Q11.
Successive increase of 10% and 20%. Find net % increase.

**Answer:**  
10 + 20 + (10×20/100)  
= 32%  

---

## Q12.
Successive decrease of 10% and 20%. Net % decrease?

**Answer:**  
10 + 20 − (10×20/100)  
= 28%  

---

## Q13.

**Answer:**  
25/125 × 100  
= 20%

# 🔴 VERY HARD LEVEL (31–40)

## Q31.
A number increased by 10%, 20%, 30%. Net increase?

**Answer:**  
10 + 20 + 30  
+ (10×20/100) + (20×30/100) + (10×30/100)  
+ (10×20×30/10000)  
= 71.6% approx
