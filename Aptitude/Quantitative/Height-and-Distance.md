## 📌 Important Formulae

- sin θ = Perpendicular / Hypotenuse  
- cos θ = Base / Hypotenuse  
- tan θ = Perpendicular / Base  
- tan 30° = 1/√3  
- tan 45° = 1  
- tan 60° = √3

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
Angle of elevation = 45°.  
Distance from tower = 20 m.  
Find height.

**Answer:**  
tan45° = h/20  
1 = h/20  
h = 20 m  

---

## Q2.
Angle of elevation = 30°.  
Distance = 10√3 m.  
Find height.

**Answer:**  
tan30° = h/(10√3)  
1/√3 = h/(10√3)  
h = 10 m
