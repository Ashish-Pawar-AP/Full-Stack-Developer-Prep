## 📌 Important Formulae

- Speed = Distance / Time  
- Relative Speed (same direction) = |S₁ − S₂|  
- Relative Speed (opposite direction) = S₁ + S₂  
- Time to cross object = (Length of train + Length of object) / Speed  

(1 km/hr = 5/18 m/s)

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
A train running at 60 km/hr crosses a pole in 6 seconds. Find length of train.

**Answer:**  
Speed = 60 × 5/18 = 16.67 m/s  

Length = 16.67 × 6  
= 100 m  

---
