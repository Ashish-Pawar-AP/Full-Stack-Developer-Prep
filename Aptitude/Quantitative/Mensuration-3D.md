# 📦 Mensuration (3D) – Interview Aptitude Practice Set  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)  
Each section contains 10 Questions with Answers.

---

## 📌 Important Formulae

- Cube → Volume = a³, TSA = 6a²  
- Cuboid → Volume = lbh, TSA = 2(lb + bh + hl)  
- Cylinder → Volume = πr²h, CSA = 2πrh  
- Cone → Volume = 1/3 πr²h  
- Sphere → Volume = 4/3 πr³, Surface Area = 4πr²  
(Use π = 22/7 unless mentioned)

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
Find volume of cube of side 5 cm.

**Answer:**  
Volume = 5³ = 125 cm³  

---

## Q2.
Find total surface area of cube of side 4 cm.

**Answer:**  
TSA = 6 × 16 = 96 cm²  

---

## Q3.

**Answer:**

## Q4.
Find volume of cylinder radius 7 cm, height 10 cm.

**Answer:**

**Answer:**
