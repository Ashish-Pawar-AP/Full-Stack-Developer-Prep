# 📊 AVERAGE – Interview Aptitude Practice Set  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

## Q1.
Find the average of 12, 18, 20, 25, 30.

**Answer:**  
Sum = 105  
Average = 105 / 5 = 21  

---

## Q2.
The average of 8 numbers is 15. Find the total sum.

**Answer:**  
Sum = 15 × 8 = 120  

---

## Q3.
The average age of 5 students is 16. What is the total age?

**Answer:**  
Total = 16 × 5 = 80  

---

## Q4.
Find the average of first 10 natural numbers.

**Answer:**  
Average = (First + Last) / 2  
= (1 + 10) / 2 = 5.5  

---

## Q5.
The average of 4 numbers is 25. If three numbers are 20, 30, 40, find the fourth number.

**Answer:**  
Total sum = 25 × 4 = 100  
Sum of 3 numbers = 90  
Fourth number = 10  

---

## Q6.
Average of 6 numbers is 12. If one number is removed, average becomes 10. Find removed number.

**Answer:**  
Original sum = 6 × 12 = 72  
New sum = 5 × 10 = 50  
Removed number = 22  

---

## Q7.
Find average of even numbers from 2 to 20.

**Answer:**  
Average = (First + Last)/2  
= (2 + 20)/2 = 11  

---

## Q8.
Average of 3 numbers is 40. If two numbers are 35 and 45, find third.

**Answer:**  
Total = 120  
Third = 120 − 80 = 40  

---

## Q9.
The average of 5 consecutive numbers is 18. Find the numbers.

**Answer:**  
Middle number = 18  
Numbers = 16, 17, 18, 19, 20  

---

## Q10.
Find average of 5, 10, 15, 20, 25.

**Answer:**  
Sum = 75  
Average = 75/5 = 15

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (11–20)

## Q11.

**Answer:**  
Total = 500  
Remaining sum = 420

## Q12.
Average marks of 6 subjects is 70. To score 75 average, how many extra marks needed total?

**Answer:**  
Required total = 75 × 6 = 450

## Q13.
The average weight of 8 persons increases by 2 kg when a new person joins. Find new person’s weight.

**Answer:**  
Increase in total = 8 × 2 = 16  
New person's weight = Old average + 16  
= 16 kg more than old avg
