# 🧮 Aptitude Arithmetic Questions (Easy → Very Hard)

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL

## Q1. Percentage
What is 20% of 250?

**Answer:**
20% of 250 = (20/100) × 250  
= 50  

---

## Q2. Profit & Loss
A product costs ₹500 and is sold for ₹600. Find the profit.

**Answer:**
Profit = SP − CP  
= 600 − 500  
= ₹100  

---

## Q3. Simple Interest
Find SI on ₹1000 at 10% per annum for 2 years.

**Answer:**
SI = (P × R × T) / 100  
= (1000 × 10 × 2) / 100  
= ₹200  

---

## Q4. Ratio
Divide 60 in the ratio 2:3.

**Answer:**
Total ratio = 2 + 3 = 5  

First part = (2/5) × 60 = 24  
Second part = (3/5) × 60 = 36  

---

## Q5. Average
Find the average of 10, 20, 30, 40.

**Answer:**
Sum = 100  
Average = 100 / 4  
= 25

## Q7. Profit %
A shopkeeper buys an item for ₹800 and sells it for ₹920. Find profit %.

**Answer:**
Profit = 920 − 800 = 120  

Profit % = (120 / 800) × 100  
= 15%  

---

## Q8. Compound Interest
Find CI on ₹2000 at 10% per annum for 2 years.

**Answer:**
Amount = 2000(1.1)²  
= 2000 × 1.21  
= 2420  

CI = 2420 − 2000  
= ₹420

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL

## Q11. Time & Work
A can do work in 12 days and B in 18 days. In how many days will they finish together?

**Answer:**
A's 1 day work = 1/12  
B's 1 day work = 1/18  

Total work/day = (3 + 2) / 36  
= 5/36  

Time = 36/5  
= 7.2 days  

---

## Q12. Mixture
In what ratio must rice at ₹40/kg be mixed with rice at ₹60/kg to get ₹50/kg?

**Answer:**
Using allegation:  

60 − 50 = 10  
50 − 40 = 10  

Ratio = 10 : 10  
= 1 : 1

## Q13. Percentage Decrease
The price of a product decreases from ₹800 to ₹680. Find percentage decrease.

**Answer:**
Decrease = 120  

% decrease = (120 / 800) × 100  
= 15%

## Q14. Boats & Streams
Speed in still water = 12 km/hr  
Stream speed = 3 km/hr  

Find downstream speed.

**Answer:**
Downstream = 12 + 3  
= 15 km/hr  

---

## Q15. Average Speed
A man travels 60 km at 30 km/hr and 60 km at 60 km/hr. Find average speed.

**Answer:**
Total distance = 120 km  

Time1 = 60/30 = 2 hr  
Time2 = 60/60 = 1 hr  

Total time = 3 hr  

Average speed = 120 / 3  
= 40 km/hr

# 🔴 VERY HARD LEVEL

## Q16. Partnership
A invests ₹10,000 for 12 months.  
B invests ₹15,000 for 8 months.  
Profit = ₹5,600. Find B's share.

**Answer:**
A's share = 10000 × 12 = 120000  
B's share = 15000 × 8 = 120000  

Ratio = 1 : 1  

Each gets = 2800

## Q17. Time & Work
A can do work in 15 days. B is 50% more efficient than A. In how many days can B do the work?

**Answer:**
If A = 15 days  

Efficiency ratio = 2 : 3  

So B's days = 15 × (2/3)  
= 10 days  

---

## Q18. Successive Discount
An item marked ₹2000 is given 20% and 10% successive discount. Find final price.

**Answer:**
After 20% discount = 2000 × 0.8 = 1600  

After 10% discount = 1600 × 0.9  
= 1440  

---

## Q19. Simple & Compound Difference
Find difference between CI and SI on ₹5000 at 10% for 2 years.

**Answer:**
SI = (5000 × 10 × 2)/100 = 1000  

CI = 5000(1.1)² − 5000  
= 5000(1.21 − 1)  
= 1050  

Difference = 50  

---

## Q20. Pipes & Cistern
Pipe A fills tank in 20 hrs.  
Pipe B fills in 30 hrs.  

Together?

**Answer:**
1/20 + 1/30  

= (3 + 2)/60  
= 5/60  
= 1/12  

Time = 12 hrs
