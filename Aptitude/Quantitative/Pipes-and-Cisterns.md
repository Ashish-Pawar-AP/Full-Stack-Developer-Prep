## 📌 Important Concept

- If a pipe fills tank in x hours → 1 hour work = 1/x  
- If a pipe empties tank in y hours → 1 hour work = −1/y  
- Net work = Sum of individual works

# 🟡 MEDIUM LEVEL (11–20)

## Q11.
Pipe A fills in 12 hours.  
Pipe B fills in 18 hours.  
Together?

**Answer:**  
1/12 + 1/18  
= 3/36 + 2/36  
= 5/36  
Time = 36/5  
= 7.2 hours  

---

## Q12.
Pipe fills in 10 hours.  
Leak empties in 20 hours.  
Net time?

**Answer:**  
1/10 − 1/20  
= 1/20  
Time = 20 hours  

---

## Q13.
Pipe A fills in 8 hours.  
Pipe B empties in 12 hours.  
Together?

**Answer:**  
1/8 − 1/12  
= 3/24 − 2/24  
= 1/24  
Time = 24 hours  

---

## Q14.
Pipe fills in 6 hours.  
After 2 hours leak opened empties in 12 hours.  
Total time?

**Answer:**  
Filled in 2 hours = 2/6 = 1/3  

Remaining = 2/3  

Net rate = 1/6 − 1/12  
= 1/12  

Time = (2/3) ÷ (1/12)  
= 8 hours  

Total = 10 hours  

---

## Q15.
Pipe A fills in 4 hrs.  
Pipe B fills in 6 hrs.  
How long for ¾ tank?

**Answer:**  
Rate = 1/4 + 1/6  
= 5/12  

Time = (3/4) ÷ (5/12)  
= 9/5  
= 1.8 hrs

# 🔵 HARD LEVEL (21–30)

## Q21.
Pipe A fills in 10 hrs.  
Pipe B fills in 15 hrs.  
Pipe C empties in 30 hrs.  
Together?

**Answer:**  
1/10 + 1/15 − 1/30  
= 3/30 + 2/30 − 1/30  
= 4/30  
= 2/15  

Time = 15/2  
= 7.5 hrs  

---

## Q22.
Two pipes fill in 12 and 18 hrs.  
Leak empties in 36 hrs.  
Time?

**Answer:**  
1/12 + 1/18 − 1/36  
= 3/36 + 2/36 − 1/36  
= 4/36  
= 1/9  
Time = 9 hrs  

---

## Q23.
Pipe fills in 8 hrs.  
After 4 hrs leak opened empties in 16 hrs.  
Total time?

**Answer:**  
Filled in 4 hrs = 1/2  

Remaining 1/2  

Net rate = 1/8 − 1/16  
= 1/16  

Time = (1/2)/(1/16)  
= 8 hrs  

Total = 12 hrs  

---

## Q24.

= 9/24  
= 3/8  

Time = 8/3 hrs  

---
