# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

### 📌 Data Table: Sales (in ₹ lakhs)

| Year | Sales |
|------|-------|
| 2019 | 50    |
| 2020 | 60    |
| 2021 | 75    |
| 2022 | 90    |
| 2023 | 100   |

---

## Q1.
What is the total sales from 2019 to 2023?

**Answer:**  
Total = 50 + 60 + 75 + 90 + 100 = 375  

---

## Q2.
Find the average sales.

**Answer:**  
Average = 375 / 5 = 75  

---

## Q3.
In which year were sales highest?

**Answer:**  
2023  

---

## Q4.
Increase in sales from 2020 to 2021?

**Answer:**  
75 − 60 = 15  

---

## Q5.
Percentage increase from 2019 to 2020?

**Answer:**  
(10/50) × 100 = 20%  

---

## Q6.
Difference between 2023 and 2019?

**Answer:**  
100 − 50 = 50  

---

## Q7.
Which year had sales equal to average?

**Answer:**  
2021  

---

## Q8.
What percent of total sales occurred in 2023?

**Answer:**  
(100/375) × 100 = 26.67%  

---

## Q9.
Find ratio of sales 2019 to 2023.

**Answer:**  
50 : 100 = 1 : 2  

---

## Q10.
Find median sales value.

**Answer:**  
75
