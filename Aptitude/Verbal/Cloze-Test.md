## ✅ Passage 1

Success does not come overnight. It requires hard work, patience, and a strong ___(1)___. Many people give up when they face ___(2)___. However, those who remain ___(3)___ and consistent eventually achieve their ___(4)___. One must always believe in ___(5)___.

**Options:**

1. (A) dream (B) anger (C) noise (D) fear  
2. (A) happiness (B) difficulties (C) success (D) rewards  
3. (A) lazy (B) negative (C) determined (D) confused  
4. (A) goals (B) problems (C) failures (D) doubts  
5. (A) others (B) luck (C) self (D) excuses  

**Answers:**  
1-A  
2-B  
3-C  
4-A  
5-C
