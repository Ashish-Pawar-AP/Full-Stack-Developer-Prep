# 🟢 1️⃣ Parts of Speech (Very Important)

### 1. Noun  
Name of a person, place, thing, idea  
👉 Ram, India, happiness  

### 2. Pronoun  
Replaces a noun  
👉 He, she, it, they  

### 3. Verb  
Action word  
👉 Run, eat, study  

### 4. Adjective  
Describes noun  
👉 Beautiful girl  

### 5. Adverb  
Describes verb/adjective  
👉 Runs quickly  

### 6. Preposition  
Shows relation  
👉 In, on, at, to, from  

### 7. Conjunction  
Joins words/sentences  
👉 And, but, because  

### 8. Interjection  
Expresses emotion  
👉 Oh! Wow! Alas!  

---

# 🟢 2️⃣ Sentence Structure

Basic format:

👉 Subject + Verb + Object  

Example:  
She plays cricket.  

Types of Sentences:
- Assertive  
- Interrogative  
- Imperative  
- Exclamatory  

---

# 🟢 3️⃣ Subject–Verb Agreement

✔ Singular → Singular verb  
✔ Plural → Plural verb  

She goes  
They go  

Special cases:
- Each / Every → Singular  
- A number of → Plural  
- The number of → Singular  

---

# 🟢 4️⃣ Tense Basics

### Present
I play  
I am playing  
I have played  

### Past
I played  
I was playing  
I had played  

### Future
I will play  
I will be playing  
I will have played  

---

# 🟢 5️⃣ Articles (A, An, The)

A → Consonant sound  
An → Vowel sound  
The → Specific  

Example:
A boy  
An apple  
The sun
