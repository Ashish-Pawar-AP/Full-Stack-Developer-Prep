# 🗣️ Aptitude Verbal – Direct & Indirect Speech  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)

---

## 📌 Basic Rules

1️⃣ Remove quotation marks in indirect speech.  
2️⃣ Change pronouns according to subject.  
3️⃣ Change tense (if reporting verb is past).  
4️⃣ Change time/place words:

| Direct | Indirect |
|--------|----------|
| now | then |
| today | that day |
| tomorrow | the next day |
| yesterday | the previous day |
| here | there |

## Q23.
