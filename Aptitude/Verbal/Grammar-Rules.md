# 📘 Aptitude Verbal – Important Grammar Rules (Quick Revision Notes)

These rules are very important for:
- Error Spotting  
- Fill in the Blanks  
- Sentence Improvement  
- Competitive Exams (Banking, SSC, Placement)

---

# 🟢 1️⃣ Subject–Verb Agreement

### ✅ Rule 1:
Singular subject → Singular verb  
Plural subject → Plural verb  

- She **goes** to school.  
- They **go** to school.  

---

### ✅ Rule 2:
Each / Every / Either / Neither → Singular verb  

- Each student **is** present.  
- Neither boy **is** ready.  

---

### ✅ Rule 3:
“The number of” → Singular  
“A number of” → Plural  

- The number of students **is** increasing.  
- A number of students **are** absent.  

---

### ✅ Rule 4:
Along with / As well as / Together with → Verb agrees with first subject  

- The manager along with his team **is** coming.  

---

# 🟢 2️⃣ Tense Rules

### ✅ Rule 5:
Since → Specific time  
For → Period of time  

- Since 2020  
- For five years  

---

### ✅ Rule 6:
Past time words → Past tense  

- Yesterday  
- Last week  
- Ago  

❌ I have completed yesterday  
✅ I completed yesterday  

---

### ✅ Rule 7:
If clause (Second Conditional)

If + Past tense → would + base verb  

- If I were you, I would study.  

---

# 🟢 3️⃣ Articles (A, An, The)

### ✅ Rule 8:
A → Consonant sound  
An → Vowel sound  

- A university (yu sound)  
- An hour (silent h)  

---

### ✅ Rule 9:
“The” used for specific things  

- The sun  
- The Taj Mahal  

---

# 🟢 4️⃣ Preposition Rules

### ✅ Rule 10:
Good at  
Interested in  
Married to  
Afraid of  
Capable of  

---

### ✅ Rule 11:
Senior to  
Junior to  
Superior to  
Prefer A to B  

---

# 🟢 5️⃣ Modals

### ✅ Rule 12:
After modal → Base form  

- Can go  
- Should study  
- Must complete  

---

### ✅ Rule 13:
Would rather → Base verb  

- I would rather stay home.  

---

# 🟢 6️⃣ Adjective & Adverb Rules

### ✅ Rule 14:
Comparative → Than  

- She is taller than me.  

---

### ✅ Rule 15:
Too…to  

- He is too weak to walk.  

---

### ✅ Rule 16:
So…that  

- He was so tired that he slept early.  

---

# 🟢 7️⃣ Gerund & Infinitive

### ✅ Rule 17:
Preposition + Verb + ing  

- Interested in learning  
- Capable of solving  

---

### ✅ Rule 18:
Look forward to + ing  

- I look forward to meeting you.  

---

### ✅ Rule 19:
Avoid / Enjoy / Deny → Verb + ing  

- He denied taking money.  

---

# 🟢 8️⃣ Conjunction Rules

### ✅ Rule 20:
No sooner…than  

- No sooner did he arrive than it started raining.  

---

### ✅ Rule 21:
Hardly…when  

- Hardly had I reached when the train left.  

---

### ✅ Rule 22:
Not only…but also  

- She not only sings but also dances.  

---

# 🟢 9️⃣ Common Confusing Words

| Wrong | Correct |
|-------|---------|
| Discuss about | Discuss |
| Return back | Return |
| Repeat again | Repeat |
| More better | Better |
| Very unique | Unique |

---

# 🟢 🔟 Voice & Speech Quick Rules

### Active → Passive  
Object + be + V3  

### Direct → Indirect  
Change tense + remove quotes + change pronouns  

---

# 🔥 Most Important Exam Tricks

1. Always check subject–verb agreement first  
2. Check preposition usage  
3. Look for tense consistency  
4. Check article usage  
5. Look for comparative errors
