# 📝 Aptitude Verbal – Error Spotting  
(Level-wise: Easy, Medium, Hard, Very Hard)

Each sentence is divided into 4 parts:  
(A), (B), (C), (D)  
Find the part that contains the error.  
If no error, choose (E) No error.

---

# 🟢 EASY LEVEL (1–10)

---

## Q1.
(A) She go  
(B) to school  
(C) every day.  
(D) regularly  

**Answer:**  
(A) → "go" should be "goes"

---

## Q2.
(A) He don't  
(B) like coffee  
(C) in the morning.  
(D)  

**Answer:**  
(A) → "don't" should be "doesn't"

---

## Q3.
(A) They was  
(B) playing  
(C) football yesterday.  
(D)  

**Answer:**  
(A) → "was" should be "were"

---

## Q4.
(A) I have  
(B) completed  
(C) my work yesterday.  
(D)  

**Answer:**  
(A) → should be "I completed"

---

## Q5.
(A) She is  
(B) more smarter  
(C) than me.  
(D)  

**Answer:**  
(B) → Remove "more"

---

## Q6.
(A) He has  
(B) two brother  
(C) and one sister.  
(D)  

**Answer:**  
(B) → "brother" should be "brothers"

---

## Q7.
(A) The sun  
(B) rise  
(C) in the east.  
(D)  

**Answer:**  
(B) → "rise" should be "rises"

---

## Q8.
(A) We are  
(B) going to  
(C) market.  
(D)  

**Answer:**  
(C) → Add "the market"

---

## Q9.
(A) She did not  
(B) went  
(C) to office.  
(D)  

**Answer:**  
(B) → "went" should be "go"

---

## Q10.
(A) He is  
(B) tallest  
(C) boy in class.  
(D)  

**Answer:**  
(B) → Add "the tallest"

---
