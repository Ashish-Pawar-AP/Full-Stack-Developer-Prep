# 🧮 QUANTITATIVE APTITUDE

---

## Q1. (TCS Pattern)
A number is increased by 10% and then by 20%. What is the total percentage increase?  
(A) 30%  
(B) 32%  
(C) 28%  
(D) 31%  

**Answer:** (B)  
(10 + 20 + 2 = 32%)

---

## Q2. (Infosys Pattern)
If A completes a work in 12 days and B in 18 days, in how many days will they complete together?  
(A) 6  
(B) 7.2  
(C) 8  
(D) 9  

**Answer:** (B)  
(1/12 + 1/18 = 5/36 → 36/5 = 7.2)

---

## Q3. (Wipro Pattern)
The average of 5 consecutive numbers is 24. What is the largest number?  
(A) 26  
(B) 27  
(C) 28  
(D) 29  

**Answer:** (A)  
(Middle = 24 → Largest = 26)
